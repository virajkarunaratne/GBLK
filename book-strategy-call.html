Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a strategy session take?

Our strategy sessions are typically 60 minutes. This gives us enough time to understand your business, discuss challenges and opportunities, and provide preliminary recommendations.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes! We offer a complimentary initial strategy session with no obligation. This is our way of ensuring we're a good fit before discussing longer-term engagements.

Can I choose between video or in-person?

Absolutely. We offer both options. Select your preference when booking, and we'll arrange the session accordingly. We're flexible based on your needs.

What if I'm not sure which service area I need?

No problem! Select "Not Sure - General Consultation" and we'll help you identify the right area during the strategy session.

How quickly will I hear back after booking?

We aim to respond within 24 hours with available time slots. Our team will work with your schedule to find the best time for your session.

What should I prepare for the call?

It's helpful to have a brief overview of your business, current challenges, and growth goals ready. However, this is not required—we'll guide the conversation based on your situation.
